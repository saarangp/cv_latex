\section*{Summary}

I’m a PhD candidate in Medical Informatics at UCLA working at the intersection of AI and neuroscience. I build generalizable foundation models for neural signals—using self-supervised methods like JEPA—to handle complex EEG and intracranial data. I also model brain network dynamics to better understand seizure evolution and movement disorders. I previously interned with the Brain AI group at Meta FAIR in London, and my work has been published at ICLR, Epilepsia, and ICASSP.
