\section*{Summary}

I am a PhD candidate in Medical Informatics at UCLA working at the intersection of AI and neuroscience. My research focuses on generalizable foundation models for neural signals — using self-supervised methods like JEPA — with applications in clinical prediction and brain-computer interfaces. I also study brain network dynamics to better understand seizure evolution and movement disorders. I previously interned with the Brain AI group at Meta FAIR in London, and my work has been published at ICLR, Epilepsia, and ICASSP, with work under review at ICML.
