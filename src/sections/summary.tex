\section*{Summary}

I recently completed my PhD in Medical Informatics at UCLA, where I built self-supervised foundation models for EEG and studied how seizure networks evolve through thalamocortical circuits. My current work combines both threads into a world model that predicts brain-state trajectories under stimulation for closed-loop neurostimulation planning. Broadly, I am interested in representation learning for biological signals and in mechanistic interpretability -- understanding what these models learn about physiology and how that can drive clinical insight. I previously worked with Meta FAIR's Brain \& AI group on multimodal neural encoding and decoding. Published at ICLR, Epilepsia, and ICASSP.
